Conduct A/B testing to improve user engagement and product performance

Manage product backlog using tools such as Jira and Productboard

Monitor KPIs including revenue growth, user acquisition, and retention

Required Skills:

Experience in Product Management

Strong understanding of Artificial Intelligence concepts

Experience with Microsoft Azure

Knowledge of A/B Testing methodologies

Experience with Power BI or similar analytics tools

Familiarity with Agile frameworks and Kanban boards

Experience using Jira or Productboard

Strong analytical and problem-solving skills

Preferred Qualifications:

Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science or related field

Experience in demand forecasting models

Experience working with cross-functional product teams
